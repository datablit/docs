# Datablit: Customer Data Platform

Datablit is an unified customer data platform for data ingestion, real-time business action, strategic business decision, persistent event intelligence and team collaboration helping you to react faster and plan smarter.

Learn how to use Datablit to collect data, deliver personalised customer experience at scale and collaborate across teams to match the speed of customer expectation.
Datablit helps you understand customers better, adapt fast and take instant business action.
